Task 1: Network Scanning with Nmap (deadline 12feb 11:59pm)

Objective: Learn how to scan a network and identify open ports.

Install Nmap and scan a local network.

Perform different types of scans:
Basic scan: nmap <IP>
Service detection: nmap -sV <IP>
Aggressive scan: nmap -A <IP>

Submit a report on your findings (What services are running? Any vulnerabilities detected?) as a document , in WEE4 day 1
